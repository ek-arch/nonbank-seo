- Title ≤ 60 chars (Google truncates at ~60)
- Meta description ≤ 155 chars (Google truncates at ~155)
- Nonbank positioning woven in where the topic legitimately overlaps with a
  differentiator (gasless, AML, self-custody, DeFi wallet + card)

## Notes for your Editor workflow

- Paste via Webflow Editor → Pages panel → click the blog post → **Page Settings → SEO**. Update `SEO Title` and `SEO Description`. Don't forget `Open Graph Title` and `Open Graph Description` on the same screen (use the same values).
- The 2024/2025 year-stamped posts are a decay risk — prioritize those.
- The gas/gasless cluster (6 posts) is your biggest topical-authority opportunity. A single hub page linking all six + the Nonbank `/gasless` differentiator page will outrank each individually.
